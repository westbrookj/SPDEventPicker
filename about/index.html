About



This web-app was created to ensure Sigma Phi Delta can uphold it's Risk Management Policy by fairly deciding who will be responsible for fulfilling each duty required per event.

Sigma Phi Delta prides itself in fostering a safe, fun, and clean environment for each and every event held!


This web-app was developed by Joshua Westbrook at the University of Missouri for his CS2830 Final Project in an attempt to streamline functions inside Sigma Phi Delta - the Engineering Fraternity he is in.
